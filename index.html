Research

I'm interested in out-of-distribution generalization and detection for robot learning. And I'm also curious about how to leverage the representation learning and causality in generative models to achieve more robust and better generalized models for robotics.

AnoF-Diff: One-Step Diffusion-Based Anomaly Detection for Forceful Tool Use
Yating Lin, Zixuan Huang, Fan Yang, Dmitry Berenson
paper

We propose a parallel one-step diffusion approach for real-time anomaly detection in forceful tool use.

Implicit Contact Diffuser: Sequential Contact Reasoning with Latent Point Cloud Diffusion
Zixuan Huang, Yinong He*, Yating Lin*, Dmitry Berenson
paper

We enable the robot to reason about the changing contacts between objects and environment learning a diffusion model over neural contact representations.

Subgoal Diffuser: Coarse-to-fine Subgoal Generation to Guide Model Predictive Control for Robot Manipulation
Zixuan Huang, Yating Lin, Fan Yang, Dmitry Berenson
paper

We use diffusion model to generate subgoals at appropriate temporal resolution dynamically to guide MPC.

Improving Out-of-Distribution Generalization of Learned Dynamics by Learning Pseudometrics and Constraint Manifolds
Yating Lin, Glen Chou, Dmitry Berenson
paper

We leverage sparsity and nonholonomic constraints to improve out-of-distribution generalization in dynamics learning.
